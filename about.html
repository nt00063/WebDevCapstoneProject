Quick FAQs

What is a “balanced plate”?

A balanced plate includes vegetables, lean protein, whole grains, and healthy fats. Aim for half veggies, one-quarter protein, and one-quarter grains.

How do I substitute ingredients?

Swap similar items: quinoa ↔ brown rice, chickpeas ↔ black beans, salmon ↔ chicken breast. Keep cooking times in mind.

Do you support dietary preferences?

Yes—many recipes include vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free suggestions directly in the instructions.
